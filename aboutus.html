CanteenGO

Reimagining Campus Dining

CanteenGO was born out of a simple observation: students spend more time standing in lines during their breaks than actually eating and relaxing. We decided to bridge this gap with technology.

Our platform enables students and faculty to browse the daily menu from classrooms, place customized food and drink pre-orders, and pick them up instantly once ready. No queues, no hassle, just good food on time.

Features of CanteenGO

Pre-Order System

Order your snacks, lunches, or beverages directly from your lecture hall or study zone.

Customizable Preferences

Tailor your drinks (sugar, ice level, size) and meals (add-ons, remarks) before ordering.

Interactive Live Tracking

Receive notifications when the kitchen accepts your order and when it is packed and ready for pick-up.

Cashless Payments

Convenient checkout supporting digital wallets like eSewa, cards, and quick cash settlement on counter.
